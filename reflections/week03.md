# Week 3 Reflection

Reflect 3: What does “authenticity” mean when identity is co-constructed with AI?
When identity is shaped with AI, It means staying in control of what you choose to say or show. AI can help with ideas or wording, but what makes something authentic is that it still reflects your own values and experiences. If you think about and edit what AI gives you instead of copying it blindly, your identity stays authentic.
