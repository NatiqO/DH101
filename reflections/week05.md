# Week 5 Reflection

Reflect 5: How does AI alter authorship and remix culture? Who owns AI-made art?
AI changes authorship because it’s no longer just one person creating. You do come up with the idea, and the AI helps shape the final result. It also pushes remix culture further since AI learns from huge amounts of existing work and blends styles in new ways. While the ownership of AI-created work and art is still being figured out, I think the person who guided the AI owns it, since they made the creative choices and directed the outcome, even if the AI helped produce the final piece.
